About Me:

I have always been interested in science and technology, as can been seen from this high quality video from my high school days.

Look at those LEDs go!

I went on to study mechanical engineering and then mixed things up and did a brief detour into the world of finance. In both courses of study, I enjoyed learning how to program. I learned how to model fluid flows and price financial assets all using MATLAB.

I then entered the professional world, but there was always a part of me that wanted to learn more about programming and computer science. I took courses on Coursera and EdX, and played around on freecodecamp.

I knew I wanted to get a formal education in computer science and in January 2020, I started a post-baccalaureate program through Oregon State University. I am around a third of the way through the program, and am excited to start working as a software engineer.

For anyone interested in seeing if computer science is for them, I highly recommend the resources in the box to the right.
